***Between Dreams***, debut album by **Nina Francis** (**October 7, 2017**). **Written by Nina Francis**; **produced by Luke Walton and Brandon Woodward** at **Tiny Giant Recording**; **mixed and mastered by [Frank Rosato](/with/frank-rosato/)** (bonus **He Was Talking to Me** recorded by Chris Sampson), [Bandcamp](https://ninafrancismusic.bandcamp.com/album/between-dreams). Eleven originals: jazzy, guitar-led singer-songwriter material in the Norah Jones orbit.

Francis is a recognized San Diego artist — [official site](https://ninafrancismusic.com/) leads with **2020 San Diego Music Awards Best Singer-Songwriter**; *Times of San Diego* also lists the win. USC Thornton Popular Music (2014) after Bishop's School; [San Diego Reader](https://www.sandiegoreader.com/bands/nina-francis/) on studying with **Randy Newman** and **Lamont Dozier**. The [April 2014 USC Thornton Pop Showcase](https://www.neontommy.com/news/2014/04/usc-thorntons-pop-showcase-highlights-seniors-troubadour.html) at the Troubadour put **FEiN**, **Nina Francis**, and **Nick Belcher** on the same senior bill, the broader USC orbit behind later [*Boomerang*](/songs/boomerang/) and this album. Post-USC: Music Box, Belly Up, Java Joe's residency.

[San Diego Troubadour](https://sandiegotroubadour.com/nina-francis-between-dreams/): “immaculately rendered… outstanding, must-have debut album”: arrangements from stark to full band around her voice. Highlights: **Cold Water**, **More**, **The Rose**, **Make Up Your Mind**, **Curiosity**, **Los Angeles**, bonus **He Was Talking to Me**. Release show **October 7, 2017** at Java Joe's, Old Town.

**Luke Francis Walton did not write *Between Dreams***: production credit only. Co-write on the earlier FEiN collab [*Boomerang*](/songs/boomerang/) (2016). Same **FEiN / Tiny Giant** era as Kerri Medders *Lot 17*.
