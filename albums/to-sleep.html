Content

to sleep (EP), fourteen short tracks, December 1, 2018, Bandcamp only (not on Spotify). Pre-$WAMI$ and pre-the DistroKid catalog that starts with Finding $D in 2019.

Bandcamp’s note: an unreleased album from 2018, an improvised lo-fi hip-hop meditation on death. Recorded at a friend’s house in LA while he was away; produced and mixed in a coffee shop in Greenwich Village. Most tracks run under ninety seconds; titles read like fragments from the same sleepless conversation — Side One, dad, are you okay, to sleep but breath abates, to sleep?, to Journey?, \/one, and the rest are all one EP, not separate releases.

Track pages link to Bandcamp for per-track streaming and download. No lyrics: improvised spoken word and beats, not annotated line-by-line. No Spotify ISRCs — Bandcamp-only, pre-DistroKid.
