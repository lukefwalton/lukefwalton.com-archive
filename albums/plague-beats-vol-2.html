Plague Beats, Vol. 2, six-track beat tape, May 14, 2021, ~one minute per track. Luke Francis Walton solo; sequel to Plague Beats, Vol. 1 (September 2020). Same pandemic-era internet-native logic as Masks and Monsters: meme titles as songs, minimal runtime, maximum title-as-content.

Five lyric tracks have annotation pages. Closer: Woah Dude: instrumental (no song-meaning page; nothing to quote). Title riffs stoner / Keanu whoa meme energy as beat-tape outro.
