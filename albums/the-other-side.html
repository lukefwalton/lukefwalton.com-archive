Content

The Other Side: 2009 EP by Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia (~17:40 per archived lukewaltonband.com). High-school blues band lineup, not The Luke Walton Band (USC cohort, 2009–2011).

Blue Suburbia (~2008–2009): Walton's third La Costa Canyon band after Casey McSocial (first band, 2006) and Mannequin (which he founded, 2006–2008). Luke Walton (guitar, vocals) · Jordan Sorokin, Ph.D. (bass) · Zach Freeman (drums). Classic rock / blues trio; won La Costa Canyon Battle of the Bands. Solo EPs Just A Friend and Heart in Hand ran in parallel with all three bands. Hub: /with/blue-suburbia/. Lineup on public record via Wikipedia and the archived discography.

Tracks: Preschool (Walton original) · Move By Yourself (~5:52, Donavon Frankenreiter cover) · Let It Rock (~3:32, Kevin Rudolf feat. Lil Wayne cover) · I Don't Need No Doctor (~4:46, John Scofield feat. John Mayer arrangement of the Ray Charles standard)

A Preschool single was also listed separately (2009, 3:40). Preschool later appeared on the Jun 2010 Charitable Chords comp as Luke Walton (Blue Suburbia) and again on Goodbye/Hello (2010).

Chronology: Just A Friend (2006) → Heart in Hand (2008) → The Other Side (2009) → Goodbye/Hello (2010).
