Content

Plague Beats, Vol. 1, six-track beat tape, September 21, 2020, ~one minute per track. Luke Francis Walton solo; same pandemic season as Masks and Monsters and Moving to Canada. Meme titles as songs, minimal runtime, maximum title-as-content, the first plague-era beat tape before Plague Beats, Vol. 2 (May 2021).

Five lyric tracks have annotation pages. Track three: Cats Are Assholes (Drums and Bass Only): instrumental (no song-meaning page); same hook as track six without vocals. Closer: Cats Are Assholes, full vocal pass, four times, QED.
