***The Other Side***: **2009** EP by **Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia** (~17:40 per [archived lukewaltonband.com](https://web.archive.org/web/20110714002356/http://lukewaltonband.com/site/CDs_and_Lyrics.html)). High-school **blues** band lineup, not **The Luke Walton Band** (USC cohort, 2009–2011).

**Blue Suburbia** (~2008–2009): Walton's third La Costa Canyon band after [**Casey McSocial**](/with/casey-mcsocial/) (first band, 2006) and **Mannequin** (which he founded, 2006–2008). **Luke Walton** (guitar, vocals) · **[Jordan Sorokin, Ph.D.](/with/jordan-sorokin/)** (bass) · **[Zach Freeman](/with/zach-freeman/)** (drums). Classic rock / blues trio; won La Costa Canyon Battle of the Bands. Solo EPs [*Just A Friend*](/albums/just-a-friend/) and [*Heart in Hand*](/albums/heart-in-hand/) ran in parallel with all three bands. Hub: [/with/blue-suburbia/](/with/blue-suburbia/). Lineup on public record via [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Luke_Walton_Band) and the archived discography.

**Tracks:** [**Preschool**](/songs/preschool/) (Walton original) · [**Move By Yourself**](/songs/move-by-yourself/) (~5:52, **Donavon Frankenreiter** cover) · [**Let It Rock**](/songs/let-it-rock/) (~3:32, **Kevin Rudolf** feat. **Lil Wayne** cover) · [**I Don't Need No Doctor**](/songs/i-dont-need-no-doctor/) (~4:46, **John Scofield** feat. **John Mayer** arrangement of the **Ray Charles** standard)

A **Preschool** single was also listed separately (**2009**, 3:40). **Preschool** later appeared on the Jun 2010 [Charitable Chords](https://charitablechords.bandcamp.com/album/know-your-rights-in-collaboration-with-knit-the-world) comp as **Luke Walton (Blue Suburbia)** and again on [*Goodbye/Hello*](/albums/goodbye-hello/) (2010).

Chronology: [*Just A Friend*](/albums/just-a-friend/) (2006) → [*Heart in Hand*](/albums/heart-in-hand/) (2008) → *The Other Side* (2009) → [*Goodbye/Hello*](/albums/goodbye-hello/) (2010).
