Garvie: October 6, 2023 on BigBoss Records. Self-produced by Garvie; mixed by Luke Francis Walton (Scoobert Doobert). Melodic Magazine and The Pentatonic name Scoobert’s mix as part of the track’s finish, including breaking Garvie’s self-imposed “no reverb” rule for a more expansive sound.

Late-2023 single before Boring (2024). No Scoobert lyrics. See With Garvie · Catalog
