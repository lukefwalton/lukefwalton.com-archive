Track seven on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), **feat. Nick Belcher**. Luke wrote, played, mixed, and mastered; Belcher sings, his second of three on the LP after [*Vegeta's Lament / Lord Frieza / Super Saiyan*](/songs/vegetas-lament-lord-frieza-super-saiyan/). Same [**Phineas and Ferb**](https://phineasandferb.fandom.com/wiki/Nick_Belcher) belter, here scoring **Goku** as messiah rather than Vegeta as opera victim.

The verse is Goku's canon savior speech to Frieza (*I am the hope of the universe... ally to good, nightmare to you*), earnest in the source material and absurd sung straight in a bedroom hip-hopera. Then the chorus turns it into a bracket: *who would you choose? / Goku or Jesus?* Twice, no resolution. Stoner theology meets Toonami christology, and the song refuses to pick. It pairs neatly with Vegeta's *you're better than me Kakarot* on the previous track: the prince surrenders, then Goku claims the cosmic job title.
