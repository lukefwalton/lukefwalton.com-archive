**Doin' It Right** is a **FEiN** single released **November 3, 2017** on [**Apple Music**](https://music.apple.com/us/album/doin-it-right-single/1306138785) (℗ **2017 Freeform**), distinct from the Tiny Giant–era singles like [*LOVED*](/songs/loved/) and [*It's Alright*](/songs/its-alright/). Co-written by **Luke Francis Walton** and **Brandon Michael Woodward**.

This is **commissioned original song work**, not a *Little Homes* outtake. Invoice and private-archive records connect the commission to **Persimmon Grove Music** — Freeform's sub-publishing / sync-catalog lane (via International Family Entertainment) — while the public receipt is the Freeform-labeled single and later screen use. See [FEiN sync & library work](/music/fein/#sync).

**Tell Me Lies** (Hulu): the same recording appears in the **series premiere (S1 E1, 2022)** per [TuneFind](https://www.tunefind.com/song/luke-francis-walton-brandon-michael-woodward/doin-it-right). Placement metadata only — not a new mix.

Not on [*Little Homes*](/albums/little-homes-fein/). Pairs with other FEiN sync rows (Lexus *Fall in Love*, *Good Trouble* on Freeform) on [/press/](/press/) and [/music/fein/#sync](/music/fein/#sync).
