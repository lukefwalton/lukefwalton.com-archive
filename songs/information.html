Track five on KŌAN, instrumental interlude between SHIAWASE NO IMI and Keep Calm Atomic Bomb. Also issued as a standalone single on streaming.

KŌAN (2022) threads game-console nostalgia, J-pop features, and spatial/binaural experiments, instrumentals are deliberate palette cleansers in the LP sequence. Luke Francis Walton wrote, produced, and mixed the record.
