Content

Track one on Plague Beats, Vol. 2 (May 14, 2021), one minute, Luke solo; sequel to Plague Beats, Vol. 1 (2020). The title is the entire concept: a loop that claims to improve each pass. One unit (better every loop / b-better every loop / yeah), sixteen times, the stutter doing breakbeat duty as loop restart.

The joke is that repetition is supposed to get better and never bothers to prove it. Vol. 2 continues with Instant Regret, Cringe, Looking Kinda Sus (Among Us), and Shitty Robots.
