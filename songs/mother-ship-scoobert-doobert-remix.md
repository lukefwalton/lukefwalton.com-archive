Scoobert Doobert remix of **bed’s** **“mother ship”** (**April 2023** single). **bed** formed in **2022** as a Tokyo underground rave-rock/post-punk unit (UNE, SINJI, JONEU, SHUN-1), live-first, anti-industry, 2052 cyberpunk world-building rather than a normal release cycle. Public singles ran **APOLOGIZE** (July 2022) → **Kare Wa** → **Michael Mann** → **mother ship** before [*Archives : May 27th, 2023*](https://friendship.mu/en/release/archives-may-27th-2023/), their first album-length drop, intentionally a live archive ([EYESCREAM](https://eyescream.jp/music/136330/)) not a “1st album.”

[Crack](https://crackmagazine.net/article/profiles/bed-tokyo-band-interview/) and [GATA](https://gatamagazine.com/articles/music/bed-tokyos-post-punk-renegades) frame the live scene: techno kicks under big riffs, bedroom parties at Shibuya clubasia, warehouse-scale raves. Walton’s later credit on [**Kare Wa 3.0**](/songs/bed-kare-wa/) (**2025** Fuji TV *HEART ATTACK* OP: mixing and additional production) sits inside that same mythos.

**bed** is not affiliated with CHAI/OKAME, only linked here via Luke’s Japan production credits.

No original lyrics, bed wrote “mother ship.” See [With bed](/with/bed/) · [Catalog](/catalog/#remixes)
