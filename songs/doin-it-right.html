Content

Doin' It Right is a FEiN single released November 3, 2017 on Apple Music (℗ 2017 Freeform), distinct from the Tiny Giant–era singles like LOVED and It's Alright. Co-written by Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward.

This is commissioned original song work, not a Little Homes outtake. Invoice and private-archive records connect the commission to Persimmon Grove Music — Freeform's sub-publishing / sync-catalog lane (via International Family Entertainment) — while the public receipt is the Freeform-labeled single and later screen use. See FEiN sync & library work.

Tell Me Lies (Hulu): the same recording appears in the series premiere (S1 E1, 2022) per TuneFind. Placement metadata only — not a new mix.

Not on Little Homes. Pairs with other FEiN sync rows (Lexus Fall in Love, Good Trouble on Freeform) on /press/ and /music/fein/#sync.
