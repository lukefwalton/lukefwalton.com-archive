Content

Tamtam (Reem Altamimi), co-written with Jordan Benjamin (Grandson); produced by Brandon Woodward and Luke Walton (FEiN), before the Scoobert bedroom-pop breakout. Video directed by Meshal Al Jaser.

Genius, FEKR’s Meet Tamtam feature, and the official MV description all name Luke Walton and Brandon Woodward as producers (Genius and FEKR also frame them as FEiN); lyrics Tamtam & Grandson; mixed Frank Rosato; mastered Riley Knapp; video Meshal Al Jaser. Production is public record. Luke Walton remembers playing on the record; unlike ROLLERCOASTER (2023), Rise does not surface instrumentation in Apple/Spotify credits metadata — that role stays private archive / memory.

Kuwait Rising (April 27, 2018): Luke Francis Walton performed live with Tamtam — named in the Lyn Winter Rise press release (Aug 2018). Separate from the studio played-on memory above.
