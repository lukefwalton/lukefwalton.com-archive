Content

Streaming version of Running with the Shaggy (feat. Babidi) from $WAMI$, track seventeen on the 2018 collage LP, Babidi on featured vocals.

This is not a proper remix session. Luke Francis Walton was messing around with the Amazing Slow Downer app on the original master and liked the warped result enough that it ended up on distro: pitch/time-stretch artifacts, lo-fi science-fair energy, same lore-dump era as the rest of $WAMI$. For the actual song placement and Babidi feature context, see the original track page and $WAMI$ album notes.
