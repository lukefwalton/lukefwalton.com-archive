Content

Track five on MÖB, a theology bit under two minutes, spoken-sung like a shower thought that won't leave. What if heaven is full of aliens all arguing their religion was the correct one, and they're kinda sexy, and wait, might I be an alien to them? The chorus raises the stakes without settling them (if we all go to heaven, what then?) before the Alf) punchline turns 80s sitcom into eschatology: if dogs go to heaven, then why can't Alf?

It sits after DMV on an album that keeps cycling anxiety, memory, and silly observation. Not a koan with an answer, a question with a joke attached.
