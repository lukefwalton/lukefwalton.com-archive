Standalone single with **Limón Limón**: LA “future indie” duo **Jason + Rand** ([Bandcamp](https://limonlimonmusic.bandcamp.com/)). [York Calling](https://yorkcalling.co.uk/2023/05/27/new-music-limon-limon-x-scoobert-doobert-300/) on airy, Moon Safari–leaning indie pop: acoustic meets ethereal, image-laden lyrics. The track also appears on their 2024 debut album [**Monterrey**](https://limonlimonmusic.bandcamp.com/album/monterrey) alongside [**Hello**](/songs/hello/).

Luke Francis Walton on the Scoobert side: **collaboration / feature**, not production for Limón Limón. Third-party remix: [3:00: Alpas, Helius & Sarco Remix](/songs/3-00-alpas-helius-sarco-remix/) (2024).

**Radio (separate from *Gonna Go to Japan*):** [FluxFM](https://www.fluxfm.de/playlist/)'s playlist archive for **6 June 2024** lists Limón & Scoobert Doobert's **3:00** at **01:38** (Berlin). A nearby Scoobert radio fossil, not the Moonlight Beach Japan track.
