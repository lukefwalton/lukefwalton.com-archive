Track two on [*Heart in Hand*](/albums/heart-in-hand/) (**2008**). **Out of (This) Place**, not the EP title; the EP is *Heart in Hand*.

Scene-setting verse: LA flash, souls sold *to be a small part of the whole*. The refrain is rescue from enclosure: *behind this glass*: toward somewhere *deeper than this empty space*. Bridge names the social trap: *so-called friends*, hurt traded as habit, *we trade hate for love 'cause we're afraid of the world outside of this place.*
