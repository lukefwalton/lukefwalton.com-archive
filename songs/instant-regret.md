Track two on **Plague Beats, Vol. 2** (**May 14, 2021**), one minute, Luke solo. Title riffs the [**Instant Regret**](https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/instant-regret-clicking-on-this-link) fail-compilation meme: *when you do some dumb shit / you get what you get*, consequence without lecture.

The chorus chants *instant regret* until it decays into *et / et / et*, regret shortened to syllable debris, the same language-collapse trick as [*Decreasingly Verbose (Ya'll'd'n't've)*](/songs/decreasingly-verbose-yalldntve/). The last stack cuts off after three, abrupt like the dumb shit itself. Sits between [*Better Every Loop*](/songs/better-every-loop/) and [*Looking Kinda Sus (Among Us)*](/songs/looking-kinda-sus-among-us/).
