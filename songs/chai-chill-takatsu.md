**Chill Takatsu**: **CHAI × TAKATSU-KING** (**TAKATSU-KING** is [Shingo Murakami](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shingo_Murakami) of SUPER EIGHT / Kanjani Eight). Luke Francis Walton **produced**.

Released for **television only**, not on CD or commercial streaming. Same production era as [WHOLE](/songs/chai-whole/), [夢のはなし (MY DREAM)](/songs/chai-yume-no-hanashi/), and [**ラブじゃん（ That's Love）**](/songs/chai-thats-love/); [Illustrate Magazine](https://illustratemagazine.com/scoobert-doobert-release-a-new-single-who-am-i-really-fooling-anyway/) (2022) names the CHAI and Murakami thread alongside *Who Am I Really Fooling Anyway*.

CHAI and Murakami also share a **separate** official collab Walton did not produce: **だれかおるやろ feat. CHAI** — theme for [*Murakami Shingo no Dareka Oru Yaro!*](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%9D%91%E4%B8%8A%E4%BF%A1%E4%BA%94%E3%81%AE%E3%81%A0%E3%82%8C%E3%81%8B%E3%81%8A%E3%82%8B%E3%82%84%E3%82%8D!) on Lemino (2023). TAKATSU-KING performs; MANA and KANA wrote; CHAI featured. Unreleased on standard albums. See [/with/chai/#murakami](/with/chai/#murakami).

No Scoobert lyrics. No public stream, catalog credit only. See [With CHAI](/with/chai/) · [Catalog](/catalog/#production)
