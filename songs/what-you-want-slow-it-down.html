Julia Ryan — What You Want (Slow It Down) (SoundCloud, uploaded August 13, 2016 · tagged Soul Pop). USC Thornton Popular Music orbit collab with FEiN (Luke Francis Walton + Brandon Woodward): produced and recorded by FEiN; guitars by Luke Walton — session credits confirmed by Walton; the guitar part is unmistakably his on the upload. The public SoundCloud description is Julia's songwriting note about manifesting self-love before giving love to someone else (slow it down until your own inner love is solid).

Same session lane as Saints And Sinners, Dear Abby, Boomerang (with FEiN), and the broader catalog — Tiny Giant / FEiN credited work.
