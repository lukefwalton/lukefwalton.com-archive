Track eleven on Big Hug, Scoobert’s cover of The Supremes’ “Where Did Our Love Go.” The LP is the 2021 attention inflection point: post-pandemic alt-pop where the weirdness becomes approachable. A Motown cover beside originals like Don't Worry and I'm an Idiot shows the project learning classic pop hooks without pretending to be a covers act.
