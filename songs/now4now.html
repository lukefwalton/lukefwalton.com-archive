Content

now4now, standalone single in the post-I stretch of the MÖBIUS cycle (MÖB → I → US → MÖBIUS). Streaming credits list GrimesAI alongside Scoobert Doobert, a vocal built with Grimes’ open elf.tech / GrimesAI-1 platform after she invited collaborators to use AI models of her voice with a royalty split (Rolling Stone on the AI-vocal moment).

Luke documented the process on Love Music More: Making a Song with GrimesAI (AI Vocals). Listed in Catalog: non-album singles.
