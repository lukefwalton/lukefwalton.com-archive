Content

Track fourteen on $WAMI$, after What a Velma, What a Night and before I Can Show You A Whole New World (on a Magic Carpet Ride). $WAMI$ album notes credit Louis Cole on the drum-and-bass moments here and on Vegeta Is Bulma's Bad Boi: one of the few outside players on an LP Luke otherwise tracked alone in a bathrobe.

“Connections” reads as a network pun; it sits in the late-album run of the collage without being a featured-vocal cut, more groove interlude with guest drums than hip-hopera.
