Content

Track two on Moonlight Beach, released as a standalone single January 13, 2023 ahead of the LP (May 19, 2023). Luke's note on Apple: written thinking about best friends on Moonlight State Beach, beer in hand. A platonic love song for the homies.

The song stacks Encinitas joy until it tips into suspicion. Pizza cheese pull (am I in a commercial?), bonfire at your favorite break, buddy Jon house-sitting on Neptune in some rich guy's infinity pool. When hometown joy hits this hard, you start to wonder if you're in an ad. On Moonlight Beach, the answer is yes, and it's real anyway.

@beformer posted six official Spotify editorial adds — Indie Pop, Modern Psychedelia, All New Indie, Ultimate Indie, Indie Brandneu, Monday Spin — in the same caption window as follow-up single Ocean View. @beformer followed with Chilled Indie (Feb 19, 2023). @beformer added Indie Covers and Feel Good (Mar 23, 2023) alongside Dancing in the Moonlight (Beach) — the promo uses Spotify’s Indie Covers artwork featuring Remi Wolf, USC Thornton contemporary of Luke Walton. Third-party remixes: Swedish Red Elephant on the single package; N0SERV1CE (Edit/Remix/Whatever) on Bandcamp (Mar 3, 2023, @n0.serv1ce · Beformer wide release teased same spring).
