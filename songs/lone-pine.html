Content

Standalone 2018 instrumental (~1:19, SoundExchange). Pre-$WAMI$ scatter alongside Tincture, Wake from Hibernation, and Speak Easy, dozens of short distro singles between the Finding $D remaster window and the May 2018 Bandcamp LP.

Lone Pine reads as Eastern Sierra geography (the town off US-395) dropped into the Encinitas bedroom-pop notebook as a title-first interlude. Instrumental; no lyrics page.
