Content

Track 8 (closer) on OKAME’s first album DEBUT, February 4, 2026 digital release per Skream!. Pre-released as the December 2025 cap on the three-month single run (after おかしなきもち and 照れないで). Lead track on the LP is たまや.

Rooftop notes the documentary OK A ME REVOLUTION THROUGH MUSIC, one-day screening Feb 11, 2026.

Project context: DEBUT album notes · With OKAME · Catalog

The Apple Music credits pane lists Scoobert Doobert as Mixing Engineer — a verified platform mix credit, not just artist press.

TuneCore Japan / LinkCore lists Scoobert Doobert on guitar and documents December 2025 Spotify playlist captures including New Music Wednesday, Indie Japan, 元気Booster, and Best New Music: DIGLE SOUND. See Catalog: official playlists.
