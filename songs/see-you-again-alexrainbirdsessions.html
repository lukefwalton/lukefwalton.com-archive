Curator session version of see you again?, released October 16, 2024 on alexrainbirdRecords as part of the alexrainbirdSessions series (October 2024 playlist credit on Spotify). YouTube hosts Scoobert Doobert - see you again? | alexrainbirdSession; Deezer lists See You Again? (alexrainbirdSessions) with Luke Walton as lyricist on alexrainbirdSessions October 2024. Same song DNA as the I album track and the pre-LP single: talisman in Kyoto, repainted dorm doors, the bridge cycling maybe I will / I hope I will / I never will.

The arrangement shift is the point. Spotify tags it indie folk: acoustic-forward, less coastal-travel production density than the album mix. Not a Live from the Void virtual-concert cut; this is a third-party curator session for playlist discovery, in the lane of other stripped Scoobert placements (compare alexrainbirdSessions metadata vs. the album version).

Parent song note unchanged: for friends lost along the way: drift, death, distance, not one breakup plot. The question mark in the title still refuses a clean answer.
