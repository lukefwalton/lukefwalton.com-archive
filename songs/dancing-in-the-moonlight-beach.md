Track three on [*Moonlight Beach*](/albums/moonlight-beach/), Scoobert’s cover of **King Harvest’s** **“Dancing in the Moonlight,”** retitled **“(Beach)”** to tie the song to [Moonlight State Beach](https://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=659) in Encinitas. The album essay treats covers as part of the record’s social, hang-out surface: less solipsistic bedroom pop, more “come outside.”

[@beformer](https://www.instagram.com/p/CpHC2DcrPYV/) announced the cover on official **Spotify Chill Vibes** (**Feb 25, 2023**) — a major editorial playlist ahead of the LP. [@beformer](https://www.instagram.com/p/CqJOsHWv_4D/) followed with **Indie Covers** and **Feel Good** (**Mar 23, 2023**) in the same post as [*Feels So Good*](/songs/feels-so-good/). The Beformer promo uses Spotify’s **Indie Covers** playlist artwork — **Remi Wolf**, who was in USC Thornton’s Popular Music Program at the same time as Luke Walton; they knew each other at school. [FIP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIP_(radio_station)) logged plays for this version alongside [*Walk Don't Run*](/songs/walk-dont-run/) during the *Moonlight Beach* cycle.

DistroKid cover, original by King Harvest. No lyrics published here.
