Content

Track six on Moonlight Beach: a California burrito interlude with no verses to hide behind. It's time to eat a shrimp burrito until it's true.

Same coastal food geography as Feels So Good (burrito de California, guac no cost) and Alright (beach burritos bigger than your forearm), stripped to pure insistence: bur-ri-to, do do do dough, done. The palate cleanser between Tired of the Sunshine and Meteor Shower. Hang-out music that sounds like hunger.
