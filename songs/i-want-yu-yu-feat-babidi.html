Content

Track six on $WAMI$, first Babidi feature on the LP, after Romantic Capitalism and before Fragments of Sappho. Babidi (Taylor James / J MESA orbit) shows up across the 2018–2020 cartoon-rap arc: this cut, Smoke That Weed Like Scooby Doobie Doo, Adonde Eres, and the later Dragon Ball $d hip-hopera.

Luke Francis Walton wrote and produced the collage; Babidi brings featured vocals on the stoner-anime thread that would fully bloom on Dragon Ball $d. See With J MESA for the collaborator hub.
