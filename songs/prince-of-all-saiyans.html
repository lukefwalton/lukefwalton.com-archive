Content

Track two on Dragon Ball $d, feat. Babidi & Gokudaxij (Taylor James / J MESA and Eric Radloff). Luke wrote, played, mixed, and mastered; they sing the feature. Same duo as Adonde Eres on $WAMI$ and later Quarantine and Chill / Happy Birthday on Masks and Monsters.

After the peacetime blunt smoke of In Peacetime Goku Smokes Blunts, Vegeta arrives and the plot starts. The hook is parade music with announcer ad-libs over the top, and the verse compresses his whole backstory into a few lines: dead king, Frieza's genocide, Nappa, let's go kill 'em all. Saiyan ideology as bumper sticker (we don't feel no pain / just Saiyan), then exit stage right. The discipline the LP promised after $WAMI$'s lore dump.

Still no Frieza, no Goku fight, just entrance energy. Next: Oozaru Transformation / Don't Kill Him, Krillin.
