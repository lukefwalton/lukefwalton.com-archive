Content

Don't Give In: Kerri Medders (Texas-born singer-songwriter and actress; SEAL Team, Alexa & Katie). Track four on the four-song Lot 17 EP (April 17, 2017: Spotify · Apple Music, ~14 minutes). Luke Francis Walton co-wrote and produced the full EP with Brandon Woodward — Slipping, Cry, Cry, and Don't Give In are all originals from that session; How Will I Know is the Whitney Houston cover. Teenplicity on the sophomore EP's '80s-leaning pop/alternative sound and Kerri's age-17 vulnerability frame.

Authorship: Ouch! Magazine quotes Kerri: every song on Lot 17 except the Whitney cover was co-written with producers Brandon Woodward and Luke Francis (Luke Francis = Luke Francis Walton). Co-writers on the Do Not Reply score page: Kerri A. Medders, Luke Francis Walton, Brandon Michael Woodward. Cry, Cry on the same EP credits production to FEiN (Walton + Woodward).

Do Not Reply (2019) starred Medders; Don't Give In is among the film's featured music. The Lot 17 cycle preceded a High School Nation tour (Plain White T's, Drake Bell; 90,000+ per artist bio).

Luke Francis Walton: co-writer, producer, and session guitarist (with Woodward). No Scoobert vocal: Medders performs.
