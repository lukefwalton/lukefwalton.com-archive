Track four on [*The Other Side*](/albums/the-other-side/) (**2009**, ~4:46). **Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia** cover routed through **John Scofield** feat. **John Mayer**’s version on [*That's What I Say: John Scofield Plays the Music of Ray Charles*](https://music.apple.com/album/thats-what-i-say/1443121761) (**2005**), not the 1966 **Ray Charles** original ([Ashford & Simpson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/I_Don%27t_Need_No_Doctor)), though that’s the song’s root. Mayer also performed it live on [*Where the Light Is*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p4R-RrSbQ-Y) (2007).

Closes the four-track blues EP after [**Preschool**](/songs/preschool/), [**Move By Yourself**](/songs/move-by-yourself/) (Donavon Frankenreiter), and [**Let It Rock**](/songs/let-it-rock/).
