Track five on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), **feat. Babidi & Gokudaxij** (**Taylor James / J MESA** and **Eric Radloff**). Luke wrote, played, mixed, and mastered; they sing the feature. Pure entrance music: the visual album gives **Captain Ginyu** his own chapter, while the LP keeps the squad intro tight. Third Babidi & Gokudaxij feature in a row after [*Prince of All Saiyans*](/songs/prince-of-all-saiyans/) and Namek.

It's a roll call, one [member](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Dragon_Ball_characters) per grunt (*I am Recoome / I am Burter / I'm Jeice / Guldo! / Ginyu*), the team motto snapped into syllables, then Ginyu's signature creep on the hook: *snatching bodies with no sound*, body-swap as silent horror. No exposition required if you watched Toonami. (Toriyama named the whole squad after refrigerated dairy, so the menace was always a little ridiculous.)

Next: [*Vegeta's Lament / Lord Frieza / Super Saiyan*](/songs/vegetas-lament-lord-frieza-super-saiyan/), where **Nick Belcher** enters on opera and the tone shifts.
