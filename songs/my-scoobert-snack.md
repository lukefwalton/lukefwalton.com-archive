Standalone single (**2018**), before [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/) and before the character had a fully built world around him. The name does the work: Scoobert Doobert is already a Scooby-Doo-adjacent alias — *Scoobert* is what you call a scooby, *snack* is what he wants: so **My Scoobert Snack** is both a self-description and the first installment of a bit. The joke continued as [*Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2*](/songs/scoobert-snack-pt-2/), which landed on [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/) at track ten, sandwiched between *Spice Up the Bedroom With Spirit Cuffs* and [*Adonde Eres*](/songs/adonde-eres-feat-babidi-gokudaxij/), proof the name had graduated to recurring character by the time the album came out.

By 2018, *snack* was also peak internet-speak for someone attractive, which Scoobert wears without explaining. The song lives in the pre-WAMI zone: looser, less conceptually dense than the LP, but audibly the same person.

[Stereofox](https://www.stereofox.com/scoobert-doobert-my-scoobert-snack/) reviewed it: early Scoobert coverage from before the [*Big Hug*](/albums/big-hug/) era widened the press footprint. See [Catalog](/catalog/) · [Press](/press/).
