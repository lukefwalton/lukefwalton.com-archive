Standalone 2018 instrumental (~1:23, SoundExchange). One of the longer one-minute scatter cuts in the pre-$WAMI$ run: same era as Donald Trumpet, Purdie Birdie, and Speak Easy.

The title names the grid: metered time, robotic feel, bedroom-pop funk experiments before the bathrobe-recorded lore dump. Instrumental palette cleanser; no lyric annotation.
