**now4now**, standalone single in the post-[*I*](/albums/i/) stretch of the MÖBIUS cycle (*MÖB* → *I* → *US* → *MÖBIUS*). Streaming credits list **GrimesAI** alongside Scoobert Doobert, a vocal built with Grimes’ open **elf.tech** / **GrimesAI-1** platform after she invited collaborators to use AI models of her voice with a royalty split ([Rolling Stone on the AI-vocal moment](https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-features/grimes-drake-the-weeknd-ai-music-1234726521/)).

Luke documented the process on Love Music More: [*Making a Song with GrimesAI (AI Vocals)*](/love-music-more/episodes/making-a-song-with-grimesai-ai-vocals/). Listed in [Catalog: non-album singles](/catalog/#singles).
