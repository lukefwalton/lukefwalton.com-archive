Track five on **Plague Beats, Vol. 2** (**May 14, 2021**), one minute, Luke solo. [Apple Music](https://music.apple.com/us/album/s-y-robots/1567468209?i=1567468215) titles it **S\*\*\*\*y Robots**; DistroKid and this page use **Shitty Robots**.

It opens on existential junk (*self-aware and worthless*), Pinocchio inverted in the [Shitty Robots](https://www.youtube.com/user/ShittyRobots) meme lineage of deliberately bad machines, then spirals into villain-origin comedy: world domination at minimum viable quality, *the shittiest nation / with inflation*. The punchline lands the whole thing as 2021 satire: *I'll start a conspiracy / idiots all on the web will follow me / I'll name it QAnon*. Same meme-POV monologue as [*Birds With Arms*](/songs/birds-with-arms/), with broken tech in place of broken bird.
