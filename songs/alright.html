Content

Standalone single (July 11, 2025 · Beformer × indiemono), not wired to US or any in-progress LP. A post-I beach day in the same 2025 singles lane as best. day. ever..

It started as a voice note on a cliff above Moonlight Beach: California burrito, waves, and a silly call-and-response, you feeling alright? / yeah. The song keeps asking until the answer sticks. The logistics are the romance: you bring the weed, I'll bring the music, is this a holiday or a workday? (I think that it's Tuesday.) Sunroof down, UV on purpose.

The rest is beach comedy, a burrito bigger than my forearm and a request to be buried like a grunion with room for my hand to hold an ice cream. The chorus stacks alright until it's less a question than a fact.

Official music video on YouTube, also on /music/#selected-videos.

Soundville called it floating-through-the-stars soul-melt; Distromono has the burrito heart-to-heart. See also: Press · Catalog.
