Content

Untitled (fọláṣadé) (April 24, 2026, Spotify) is a J MESA solo single. The Spotify credits pane lists Luke Walton as Mixing Engineer, Riley Knapp as Mastering Engineer, and Taylor James Washington as lyricist and composer — Taylor James is J MESA on the artist credit.

Press describes Afro-Latin influence and a slow-burning contemporary R&B / soul track:

  • Aipate, groove-forward review of the release
  • Soaplife Magazine, slow-burning romance framing

Same client lane as Crying Today and I Say (I Did It Again), distinct from joint-artist collaborator receipts on Prisoner, Big Hug, and Live from the Void.

No Scoobert lyrics. See also: With J MESA · Catalog
