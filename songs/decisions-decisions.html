Self-produced by Garvie; mixed by Luke Francis Walton. @garviesounds (Nov 23, 2022): BBC Radio 1 Next Wave / Future Artists with Jack Saunders — national-radio pick for a spare-room self-production Scoobert mixed. @garviesounds out-now reel (Oct 14, 2022) tags @scoobertdoobert.pizza for the mix and @beformer for distribution. York Calling on the frenetic sub-two-minute groove: “confession that he’s not a great decision maker” mashed into pop styles that shouldn’t fit but do.

Opens the six-single Garvie mixing run through Boring (2024). No Scoobert lyrics. See With Garvie · Catalog
