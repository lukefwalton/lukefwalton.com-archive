Drive: Tamtam (Reem Altamimi), June 2018. Arab News credits lyrics by Tamtam, production by Siren, and executive producer Luke Walton: released as Saudi Arabia lifted the ban on women driving. The MV shows Tamtam and friends in a vintage yellow Mustang around Orange County.
