Content

Next Time on Dragon Ball SD (feat. Nick Belcher), vocal/feature version tied to the same DBZ SD trailer concept as Aaahh!!! Real Monsters, the instrumental closer on Finding $D and Next Time on Dragon Ball SD on $WAMI$. Nick Belcher: lead vocalist of Hammer City / The Hammers (Heartquake Saga EP, 2016), also features across Dragon Ball $d (Vegeta's Lament, Ally to Good, My Dad Sucks, etc.). This cut predates the full hip-hopera as a standalone feature.

Not on the Dragon Ball $d LP tracklist; lives as its own streaming single bridging the 2018 collage era and the 2020 cartoon-rap record.
