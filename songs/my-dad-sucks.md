Track eight on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), **feat. Gohan & Nick Belcher** on streaming. Luke wrote, played, mixed, and mastered; **Nick Belcher** sings. **Gohan** is the joke credit: [Spotify lists him](https://open.spotify.com/album/7q5q0RcMxVHR381gS3r1ri) as a featured artist, but it's a meta gag about Gohan hating Goku, not a real third vocalist. The savior of the previous track gets recast here as a deadbeat dad.

The hook is playground envy (*human fathers not from space*) over pop-punk *sorry* ad-libs, and the verse is a straight grievance list about training absences and emotional abandonment. The bridge is where the kid logic gets dark: Goku's noble self-destruction in the Cell saga becomes *when you committed suicide / you took King Kai and Bubbles' live* (the *live* spelling is the writer's, kept), and *met some girl out on Snake Way* turns afterlife errands into infidelity. The worst dad in the universe is also the strongest.
