Content

Track three on Dragon Ball $d, a Luke solo. The medley title earns its slash: Oozaru Transformation first, Don't Kill Him, Krillin second, the same way the visual album splits chapters apart on YouTube.

Part one is pure trash-talk: Galick Gun, going ape (the Great Ape Oozaru), Nappa as guarantee. Then a voice shift to Krillin and a crude, decidedly not cartoon-safe revenge monologue. The closer turns the franchise's famous plea into a real question, who would spare him? Mercy as plot beat: spare the villain or finish it.

Two saga beats, one slot, the same compression as track two. Next: Namek Has Dragon Balls! (A Chill's in the Air).
