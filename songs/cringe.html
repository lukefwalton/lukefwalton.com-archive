Content

Track three on Plague Beats, Vol. 2 (May 14, 2021), one minute, Luke solo. The entire lyric is cringe, twenty-six times.

No setup, no punchline, same form as Cats Are Assholes on Vol. 1. Here the internet's default judgment word is the beat, repeated until you feel the title: cringe as audio wallpaper, not confession. It anchors Vol. 2's middle run of one-word meme moods, between Instant Regret and Looking Kinda Sus (Among Us).
