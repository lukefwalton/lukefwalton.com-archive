Content

Track eight on MÖB, the manic permission-slip song. It opens in Saturday insomnia (leg bouncing, floor shaking, never calm down) and names the politics underneath: freedom wanting liberty, no assembly line, taking back my time. Then the coping vocabulary collapses to one word. Go to the beach, smoke weed, the boss keeps calling, go to the movies, eat another gummy, and when it gets too heavy just say fuck it.

The hook is dumb on purpose: not the beach, not the movie, bowling, four times over. The most mundane rebellion wins, and the coda admits what's actually driving it: I wanna quit my fucking job so badly. All I Need closes the album with enough; this track is what enough sounds like when you're still vibrating.
