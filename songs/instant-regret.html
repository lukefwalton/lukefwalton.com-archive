Content

Track two on Plague Beats, Vol. 2 (May 14, 2021), one minute, Luke solo. Title riffs the Instant Regret fail-compilation meme: when you do some dumb shit / you get what you get, consequence without lecture.

The chorus chants instant regret until it decays into et / et / et, regret shortened to syllable debris, the same language-collapse trick as Decreasingly Verbose (Ya'll'd'n't've). The last stack cuts off after three, abrupt like the dumb shit itself. Sits between Better Every Loop and Looking Kinda Sus (Among Us).
