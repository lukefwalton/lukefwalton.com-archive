Track 13 on to sleep (EP), December 1, 2018, Bandcamp only. Improvised lo-fi hip-hop meditation on death: recorded at a friend's house in LA, mixed in a Greenwich Village coffee shop. Most cuts run under ninety seconds; titles read like fragments from one sleepless conversation.

No lyrics: improvised spoken word and beats, not annotated line-by-line. See to sleep album notes · Catalog
