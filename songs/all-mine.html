Content

FEiN single (2017, ℗ Tiny Giant), co-written by Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward. Mixed and mastered by Frank Rosato. Closes the post-Little Homes 2017 single run with LOVED, It's Alright, Fear, and P.A.R.T.S..

A villain's monologue, extraction dressed as flirtation. Your focus is my weapon, and the chorus turns reassurance into threat: you'll be fine / till the day you drop. The title is a deed.

The interlude plays as hospitality horror, the host as predator: drink up / remember nothing / leave your questions at the door. By the outro the amnesia hardens into leave your logic at the door, body and memory and reason all surrendered. FEiN host-as-villain theater, of a piece with Lucky You (mock gratitude) and Behave (scripted loyalty).
