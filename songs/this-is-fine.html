Content

Companion to LOL on the same chapter EP. Where the title track had verses (the party dodge, foot in mouth, philosophical songs never have answers), this one keeps only the coping phrase and runs it until the narrative disappears.

It's KC Green's burning-room dog as a pop song. The middle break even fractures the line, everything is / fine / everything is. Same denial as LOL, just louder, the way AFTER-AFTER-PARTY followed AFTER-PARTY: sequel as repetition.
