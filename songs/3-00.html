Content

Standalone single with Limón Limón: LA “future indie” duo Jason + Rand (Bandcamp). York Calling on airy, Moon Safari–leaning indie pop: acoustic meets ethereal, image-laden lyrics. The track also appears on their 2024 debut album Monterrey alongside Hello.

Luke Francis Walton on the Scoobert side: collaboration / feature, not production for Limón Limón. Third-party remix: 3:00: Alpas, Helius & Sarco Remix (2024).

**Radio (separate from Gonna Go to Japan): FluxFM's playlist archive for 6 June 2024 lists Limón & Scoobert Doobert's 3:00 at 01:38** (Berlin). A nearby Scoobert radio fossil, not the Moonlight Beach Japan track.
