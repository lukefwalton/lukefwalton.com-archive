Scoobert Doobert Edit of Thumpasaurus’ “Lipstick Makeup”: September 27, 2024 (Beformer; ~2:00 edit). Luke Francis Walton reshapes the LA indie pop-funk band’s 2023 single: Thumpasaurus framed the original as saying “fuck it” and letting your freak out at the dance party (CelebMix); the edit tightens that energy for streaming.

Remix/edit credit, not a Scoobert original: Chicago funk via California production. Original appeared on Thumpasaurus’ Hard era (March 2023).
