Content

Track four on Moonlight Beach, released as a standalone single ahead of the LP. EARMILK heard laidback summer guitar, bass grooves, and calm ocean-facing pop, a preview of the whole record's posture.

A deserted-island domestic fantasy that keeps shrinking the world to fit two people. The island is not very big, so every day we go in circles, which is the whole ambition rather than a complaint.

Less joke than Shrimp Burrito, less ecstatic than Feels So Good: ocean view as enough. Pairs with Tired of the Sunshine as the album's push-pull on California light.

Also on Catalog as 2023 single.
