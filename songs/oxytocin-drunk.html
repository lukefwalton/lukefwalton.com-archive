Track 1 on applied communications has a midlife crisis, Applied Communications (Max Wood), April 26, 2024. Lead single and It's Psychedelic Baby exclusive premiere.

The EP is Wood’s comeback after 17 years, alt-pop navigating depression, gender dysphoria, and self-love (V13). Mixed by Luke Francis Walton (Scoobert Doobert): credited alongside CHAI and Victor Marc work in press; mastered by Riley Knapp.

No Scoobert lyrics. See the EP essay · Bandcamp · /press/
