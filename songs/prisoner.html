Content

Prisoner (2024) is a standalone single credited on Spotify to J MESA and Scoobert Doobert. Deezer writer/composer metadata lists Luke Francis Walton and Taylor James Washington — Taylor James is J MESA; the same person earlier appeared as Babidi on Dragon Ball $d, $WAMI$, Masks and Monsters, and the Big Hug collabs I See the Moment and Hold Up.

This is a collaborator credit, not a mixing-for-hire page. The public receipt is the joint artist line and writer metadata, not press naming Scoobert as mixer on a J MESA solo release.
