Track one on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), the hip-hopera opener and a Luke solo. **February 22, 2020** on streaming, Bandcamp **March 16, 2020**. First Grizzard Graphics cover in the Scoobert run.

The title is the whole premise: no Frieza, no apocalypse, just peacetime Goku doing what peacetime Goku would do. The saga opens in stoner minimalism rather than exposition, with the [Turtle Hermit](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Master_Roshi) recast as chill deity and planetary stakes treated as a reason to relax. No plot advances, which is the point.

It pairs with the instrumental closer [*While My Beerus Gently Sleeps*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/) as the album's chill bookends, peace before [*Prince of All Saiyans*](/songs/prince-of-all-saiyans/) and Namek wreck the blunt. The trailer on [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/) was [*Next Time on Dragon Ball SD*](/albums/wami/). This is where that tease lands.
