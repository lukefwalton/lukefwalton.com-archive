Content

Track seven on I, the drowsy-drive song that's also a love song. The title is the PSA, the verses are the confession: island loop, lighthouse on the water, the slide into zoning zoning out. The danger is comfort itself, cozy as molasses. The fix isn't caffeine, it's calling up my baby to love me back awake, a partner's voice as stimulant. The outro is the arrival it buys: laughing, soon I'm pulling up / home to you.

On an album built from train rides, hikes, and coastal motion, this is the small-circle version: island loop, partner on the phone, home as endpoint. Pairs with omw as the I travel-love spine.
