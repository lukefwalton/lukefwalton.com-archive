Scoobert Doobert remix of Jamie Drake’s “New Girl”: April 14, 2023 (Antifragile Music). Luke Francis Walton remix credit only.

@jamiethedrake announced the remix April 10, 2023 — out that Friday on @antifragilemusic · art @mollytuttledesign · pre-save. On tour after release, Jamie closed every set with a dance party to this remix.

Drake also cowrote and features on Meteor Shower from Moonlight Beach, remix and LP co-write share the same collaborator thread on jamiedrakemusic.com.
