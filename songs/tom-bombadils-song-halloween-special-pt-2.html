Content

Standalone 2018 cut (~1:02, SoundExchange). Halloween Special, Pt. 2 in the title: Tolkien's Old Tom Bombadil reduced to a trick-or-treat mantra in the pre-$WAMI$ scatter, same lab-notebook era as Finding $D remaster activity.

There is no plot. The lyric is I'm Tom Bombadil on loop (~10 passes), same insistence grammar as later Shrimp Burrito (it's time to eat a shrimp burrito until true). Pt. 1 is not indexed on this site; only this SoundExchange single surfaced.
