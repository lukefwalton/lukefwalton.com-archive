**Garvie** — “OTT DIY N64-tinged pop” ([garviesounds.com](https://garviesounds.com/)). **Self-produced by Garvie**; **mixed by Luke Francis Walton.** [@garviesounds](https://www.instagram.com/p/Ck8odk4jTEY/) (**Nov 23, 2022**): **BBC Radio 1 Next Wave** / **Future Artists** with **Jack Saunders** — national-radio pick for a spare-room self-production Scoobert mixed. [@garviesounds out-now reel](https://www.instagram.com/reel/CjsVU1LDU_l/) (**Oct 14, 2022**) tags [@scoobertdoobert.pizza](https://www.instagram.com/scoobertdoobert.pizza/) for the mix and [@beformer](https://www.instagram.com/beformer/) for distribution. [York Calling](https://yorkcalling.co.uk/2022/10/27/new-music-garvie-decisions-decisions/) on the frenetic sub-two-minute groove: “confession that he’s not a great decision maker” mashed into pop styles that shouldn’t fit but do.

Opens the six-single Garvie mixing run through [*Boring*](/songs/boring/) (2024). No Scoobert lyrics. See [With Garvie](/with/garvie/) · [Catalog](/catalog/#production)
