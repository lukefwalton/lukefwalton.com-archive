Content

Track one on KŌAN, opener of KŌAN A (March 18, 2022), full LP September 9, 2022. Luke solo on a spatial, headphone-built EP. Glasse Factory called it experimental funk, ambient layers tipping into alt-rock guitar and then an indie-pop-funk anthem. Radio premiere: @thedropwithdanno hosted a full-hour guest session on GFN (98.7 Gwangju · 93.7 Yeosu, South Korea) with @thedamyanghouse — world premiere of Think About It on February 25, 2022 (podcast replay), three weeks before KŌAN A dropped; on-air CHAI production thread · Instagram recap (Feb 27) · Spotify guest playlist · screenshot on /music/#photos. The hook works like Don't Worry on Big Hug, a command that fails on contact: don't think I'm not thinking 'bout it runs eight times, and saying it makes you do it.

Underneath the funk is a 2022 dread stack, NFTs and the end of days and should I procreate or just use my hand? The bridge condenses the record's title, Schrödinger's cat read as Zen paradox: is Schrödinger alive? well yes and no, a koan in disguise. Identity comes on loan, and if time doesn't exist we're all simultaneously eternal.

This is the front of the LP's hinge, Think About It into SHIAWASE NO IMI into Keep Calm Atomic Bomb (KŌAN essay): paradox jokes, happiness-pop, atomic dread. Pairs with 無門関 and What Makes You You on the Zen and identity thread.
