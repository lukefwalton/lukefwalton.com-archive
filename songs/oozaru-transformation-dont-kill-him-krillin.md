Track three on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), a Luke solo. The medley title earns its slash: **Oozaru Transformation** first, **Don't Kill Him, Krillin** second, the same way the [visual album](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4OaFBwv0dsw) splits chapters apart on YouTube.

Part one is pure trash-talk: Galick Gun, *going ape* (the Great Ape **Oozaru**), Nappa as guarantee. Then a voice shift to **Krillin** and a crude, decidedly not cartoon-safe revenge monologue. The closer turns the franchise's famous plea into a real question, *who would spare him?* Mercy as plot beat: spare the villain or finish it.

Two saga beats, one slot, the same compression as track two. Next: [*Namek Has Dragon Balls! (A Chill's in the Air)*](/songs/namek-has-dragon-balls-a-chills-in-the-air/).
