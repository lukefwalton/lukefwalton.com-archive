Track four on [*Plague Beats, Vol. 1*](/albums/plague-beats-vol-1/) (**September 21, 2020**), one minute, Luke solo. The title is the [**Bread Stapled to Trees**](https://knowyourmeme.com/memes/bread-stapled-to-trees) meme, the r/BreadStapledToTrees subculture turned into a chant. The hook is the whole joke, eight times, mantra rather than buildup.

In between, a sommelier's guide to vandalism breaks out: *Japanese juniper pairs well with white bread*, *sourdough on oak*, *twelve-grain on cedar*. Same sourdough brain as [*I Am a Slave to the Yeast*](/songs/i-am-a-slave-to-the-yeast/) on [*Masks and Monsters*](/albums/masks-and-monsters/), in meme register. Then eight more *bread stapled to trees*, and out.
