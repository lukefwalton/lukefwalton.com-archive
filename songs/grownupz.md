Originally **August 25, 2014** on [**FEiN Times (Issue #1)**](https://music.apple.com/us/album/fein-times-issue-1-single/907610072) with B-side [**Monay Grabba**](/songs/monay-grabba/). Track five on [*Little Homes*](/albums/little-homes-fein/) (**May 31, 2016**), after [*Outro*](/songs/outro/). [SoundCloud](https://soundcloud.com/feinmusic/grownupz) (~339k plays; [album version](https://soundcloud.com/feinmusic/grownupz-2) ~137k) · [**#Grownupz (8 Bit/Chiptune)**](https://soundcloud.com/feinmusic/grownupz_8bit) (FEiN × **Cydoniac**) · [**official music video**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Usz0siRteGI) — FEiN's only produced MV (also on [/music/fein/#videos](/music/fein/#videos) and [/music/#selected-videos](/music/#selected-videos)). Walton/Woodward co-write.

**Recording:** **Michael Christofi** tracked **#Grownupz** vocal takes inside actor [**Gary Sinise**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Sinise)'s garage — laptop and interfaces on an ironing board, red acoustic foam on the wall. Indexed **Feb 22, 2016** FEiN Facebook throwback (archivist proof: [/evidence/fein-grownupz-christofi-ironing-board.png](/evidence/fein-grownupz-christofi-ironing-board.png)): *Throwback to recording #Grownupz on an ironing board with Christofi.* That mobile vocal pass sits alongside the earlier **LMU** drum day with Christofi that seeded [*Little Homes*](/albums/little-homes-fein/) — same engineer, different room.

**Breakout:** FEiN's **#Grownupz** became the band's clearest early micro-breakout — not memory haze. [**Hype Machine**](https://hypem.com/track/25xjc/FEiN+-+Grownupz): **Dec 16, 2015** FEiN Facebook post (archivist proof: [/evidence/fein-grownupz-hype-machine-charts.png](/evidence/fein-grownupz-hype-machine-charts.png)) reports **#13 non-remix charts**, **#20 popular charts**, **#2 Twitter charts**, and **30,000+ plays**; chart screenshots match the indexed Hype Machine pages. The [track page](https://hypem.com/track/25xjc/FEiN+-+Grownupz) still indexes the song (**posted by 7 sites**; links [Fresh Beats 365](https://freshbeats365.com/2017/04/12/fein-grownupz-music-video/) and blog coverage). [**Indie Shuffle**](https://www.indieshuffle.com/fein-grownupz/) (**Dec 13, 2015**, Jason Grishkoff) shared **#Grownupz** as the duo's kickoff track (Aug 2014 Issue #1 release). [**I Heart Moosiq**](https://iheartmoosiq.tumblr.com/post/177593085294/premiere-fein-lucky-you-lucky-us-we-get) later described it as a **Spotify and Hype Machine** success, tied the arc to their earlier **#Grownupz premiere**, and indexed the path into later FEiN releases. A separate [I Heart Moosiq post](https://www.tumblr.com/iheartmoosiq/140252129934/its-been-far-too-long-since-ive-shared-fabulous-music-from) places **#Grownupz** in Spotify's **U.S. Viral top 10**; that aligns with [BMI](https://www.bmi.com/news/entry/indie_spotlight_fein) reporting **#4** on Spotify's U.S. Viral 50 and 500k+ streams by September 2016, [Canadian Press / *Globe and Mail*](https://www.theglobeandmail.com/feeds/canadian-press/entertainment/spotifys-top-10-most-viral-tracks/article28833695/) listing among Spotify's top viral tracks, and an indexed **Feb 13, 2016** FEiN Facebook artifact claiming **#4 most viral in the US** (post not fully archived here).

**Press & spread:** [Fresh Beats 365 interview](https://freshbeats365.com/2016/04/09/fein-interview/) (**Apr 9, 2016**, Tom Roden) — Brandon on sudden **#Grownupz** Spotify activity driving the *Little Little Homes* teaser (*we wanted to update our presence on our page*). [ReverbNation Blog](https://web.archive.org/web/20220628192349/https://blog.reverbnation.com/2017/01/27/spotify-playlists-dragon-ball-z-and-collabs-an-interview-with-fein/) (**Jan 27, 2017**, Dave) — *Spotify Playlists, Dragon Ball Z, and Collabs* — Luke and Brandon on Christofi × [*The Disco*](/songs/the-disco-feat-mike-funk/) and **#Grownupz** stats: *over 1 million plays on Spotify, features on several playlists, and featured on Hype Machine*; Brandon on Discover Weekly saves/repeat listens snowballing; Luke on *near the top of the Spotify Viral 50 for the US, Canada, and Mexico* and **#GZ in 50,000+ playlists*. Asked what drove the breakout, Luke: *Hashtags and the letter z. #DragonBallZ* — same public window that names Dragon Ball Z in the headline ([Dragon Ball $d spine](/catalog/#dragon-ball-spine)). [Fresh Beats 365 EP review](https://freshbeats365.com/2016/03/10/fein-little-little-homes-ep-review/) (**Mar 10, 2016**) singled out **#Grownupz** on the *Little Little Homes* teaser as bubblegum indie-pop with child-vocal warping (*not any throwaway indie-electro artist*); their [**Apr 2017 MV feature**](https://freshbeats365.com/2017/04/12/fein-grownupz-music-video/) wrote that **#Grownupz** first put FEiN on most people's radars in **2014**. Indie blog traction: [The Burning Ear](https://www.theburningear.com/fein-grownupz-girl-you-cant-hide-it/) · [Poprock Record](https://poprockrecord.com/2016/02/16/kids-today/). **College radio:** [WMBR](https://www.track-blaster.com/wmbr/playlist.php?id=25108) *Insomnia Sounds* played the **FEiN Times Issue #1** cut **Nov 20, 2016** at 12:53 a.m. Post-*Little Homes*, [*LOVED*](/songs/loved/) (2017) became the duo's biggest track overall (~9M Spotify). Later public video: [KCRW 360° sessions](/music/fein/#videos) for [*Liminal*](/songs/liminal/) and [*Stupid Forever*](/songs/stupid-forever/) — live performance, not additional MVs.

**Rivers Cuomo** ([Weezer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rivers_Cuomo)) added **#Grownupz** (from [**FEiN Times Issue #1**](/songs/monay-grabba/), not the later *Little Homes* album cut) to his public Spotify playlist [**DJ set**](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/7ecIc0b9WmaP3soJSyZPB0) — **Feb 27, 2016**, track 3 (archivist proof: [/evidence/fein-grownupz-rivers-cuomo-dj-set-spotify-2016-02-27.png](/evidence/fein-grownupz-rivers-cuomo-dj-set-spotify-2016-02-27.png)). Cuomo’s playlist note: *here's the set I played at Melissa's party* (14 songs, 51:44 · 185 saves on capture). Full party set: Alan Walker “Faded” · Future/Drake “Where Ya At” · **FEiN “#Grownupz”** · Young Thug “Best Friend” · Desiigner “Panda” · Emmit Fenn “Painting Greys” · Rae Sremmurd “No Type” · Greyson Chance “Hit & Run” · Drake/Future “Jumpman” · Módl “Years From Now” · Dawn Richard “Not Above That” · Lil Yachty “1 Night” · Marian Hill “Down” · Drake “Know Yourself”. That is a direct Cuomo ↔ FEiN link separate from the CHAI production arc — but Cuomo and [**Non**](/songs/chai-yume-no-hanashi/) (のん) later both wrote [release congratulations comments](https://natalie.mu/music/news/542174) for CHAI's self-titled album **CHAI** (Sep 22, 2023), alongside Shingo Murakami, Kaho Nakamura, Orono, and Sui Ishida. Non stars in [*The Fish Tale*](/songs/chai-yume-no-hanashi/) (さかなのこ), where Walton sound-produced CHAI's **MY DREAM** theme — a Japan network where the **#Grownupz** title is coincidence, not a shared song, but the same quirky-indie orbit.

B-side: [**Monay Grabba**](/songs/monay-grabba/).

**FEiN Times (Issue #1)**'s *Recording Journal* (Aug 2014) names **money** as the inaugural theme and pairs this song with [*Monay Grabba*](/songs/monay-grabba/): imperialism on the B-side, a *pill-poppin, jump-jumpin trust-fund baby that doesn't wanna grow up anyway* on the A. Written in practice rooms, Venice and Downtown apartments, walks to Which Wich, a *little home in Westlake*, and voice notes in traffic. The LMU drum sessions that day (with **Michael Christofi**) were meant for an EP; fourteen hours later the band had the foundation for [*Little Homes*](/albums/little-homes-fein/). *Brandon was tired.*

The joke is a rich kid weaponizing wisdom against itself. Told that desire is the root of suffering, he answers *I've got mad stacks, so desire ain't a thing*; told happiness comes from deep inside, *well I bet he's poor, I bet he live a shitty life*. Inheritance funds the regression: the father worked so he wouldn't have to, the nanny taught him the days of the week (which he then raps as a flex), and the whole thing dissolves into pills, an EDM body-count, and the title's refusal, *daddy I don't wanna grow up anyway, grown ups are all sad.* The bridge admits the cage without leaving it: *what would you do if you were me, raised in the pit of temptation and greed.*

Same thread as [*American Man*](/songs/american-man/) and [*Sculptor*](/songs/sculptor/): here youth is the brand, sadness is for grownups, and the beat keeps you from thinking about either. Leads into [*Goodness Gracious*](/songs/goodness-gracious/).
