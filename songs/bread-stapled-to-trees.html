Content

Track four on Plague Beats, Vol. 1 (September 21, 2020), one minute, Luke solo. The title is the Bread Stapled to Trees meme, the r/BreadStapledToTrees subculture turned into a chant. The hook is the whole joke, eight times, mantra rather than buildup.

In between, a sommelier's guide to vandalism breaks out: Japanese juniper pairs well with white bread, sourdough on oak, twelve-grain on cedar. Same sourdough brain as I Am a Slave to the Yeast on Masks and Monsters, in meme register. Then eight more bread stapled to trees, and out.
