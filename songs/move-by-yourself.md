Track two on [*The Other Side*](/albums/the-other-side/) (**2009**, ~5:52). **Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia** cover of **Donavon Frankenreiter**’s **“Move By Yourself”**: title track from [*Move by Yourself*](https://www.allmusic.com/album/move-by-yourself-mw0002345225) (**2006**, Lost Highway).

High-school **blues** band lineup (parallel to **Mannequin** / pre–Luke Walton Band), not **The Luke Walton Band** USC cohort. Frankenreiter’s original is surf-adjacent funk-soul independence anthem (*you've got to move by yourself*); Walton’s EP slots it between original [**Preschool**](/songs/preschool/) and two more covers.
