Track three on [*Heart in Hand*](/albums/heart-in-hand/) (**2008**).

*All alone in a crowded place / small talk is all we sing*, performance without contact. The hook is ritual: *tryin' ta fit the storyline, kow-towin' the rusty sign*, scripted romance, worn signage, *take this five times and repeat*. Verse two reframes intimacy as surface (*the skin was where it ends*). Bridge: *dead air from the side from inside of you*: tried to get through anyway.
