Content

Follows the Sub Pop remix on WINK TOGETHER — Pitchfork lists Miracle (Scoobert Doobert Remix) on the EP tracklist — and opened a long production run across CHAI's Japan catalog. @wavyawards nominated WHOLE for Collaboration of the Year at The Wavys 2022 (July 28, 2022). NME names Walton as producer on WHOLE and the NHK series _Koi-senu Futari_. Qetic (Jan 12, 2022) documents サウンドプロデュース by California-based Scoobert Doobert on the 恋せぬふたり theme 「まるごと」. Paste and FLOOD cover the single in the same release window; Sony Music Japan documents 「まるごと」 as the drama theme with Scoobert Doobert on sound production. Sub Pop Mega Mart names Scoobert Doobert as producer; SoundBetter lists producer, arranger, and mixing engineer credits on WHOLE; YouTube's official audio and MV uploads carry the public release metadata. Studio BTS: CHAI drummer Yuna posted a drum-recording reel for 「まるごと」 on January 12, 2022 — the day before release — #yunadrum #drumrecording; screenshot on /music/#photos. Anthony Fantano (The Needle Drop) reacted to WHOLE in a fan-captured clip — see /with/anthony-fantano/. Also on /music/#selected-videos and /with/chai/#videos.

Final live: CHAI disbanded after their March 12, 2024 show at EX THEATER ROPPONGI. Sony Music Japan documents the first Blu-ray 『We The CHAI Tour! FINAL ～NEO KAWAII IS FOREVER♡～』 (July 3, 2024 · SIXL-3): full last-show video (~106 min, 22 songs) plus documentary. Track 11: まるごと (WHOLE) — Walton production. Track 3: ラブじゃん (also Walton). Full setlist on /with/chai/#final-live.
