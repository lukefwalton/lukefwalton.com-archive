Content

Embody feat. FEiN (3), a Walton/Woodward topline on Embody (Karolis Labanauskas)'s single Remember Us (Extended Mix) (June 16, 2016). Apple Music credits: L.F. Walton composer, Brandon Michael Woodward songwriter/lyrics, K. Labanauskas composer. Official video. Remember Us [Re-Edit] (April 23, 2021, The Bearded Man): Apple Music credits lists Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward as vocals, plus composition credits for Walton, Woodward, and Labanauskas; K. Labanauskas producer.

Released sixteen days after Little Homes (May 31, 2016), same FEiN summer: concept LP at Tiny Giant, then a deep-house feature on Armada Deep. Radio: Don Diablo — Hexagon Radio Episode 045 (Dec 9, 2015 SoundCloud upload) opens with Embody – Remember Us (feat. FEiN) as track 1 — episode description claims broadcast in 35+ countries, plus Evolution on iHeart Radio (USA), Fun Radio (France), and SLAM FM (Netherlands). Pre-release radio support ahead of the Jun 16, 2016 Armada Deep single. See With Embody (Karolis Labanauskas).

What FEiN brought to the slot is the move the song turns on: the bridge image isn't a jump, it's the clarity from the ledge. No one will remember us is not a nihilist toast but the memento mori that buys the dance floor. The second verse refuses comfort (no need for you to comfort me / at all) and exits on at all, no chorus return. Topline as philosophy, not hook factory.
