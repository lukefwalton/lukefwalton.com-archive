Content

Track eight on Moonlight Beach: the Japan song on a California place-record. After KŌAN opened the Japan thread (Japanese lyrics, Zen paradox, spatial sound), this keeps the longing alive while Encinitas is still home base.

It's a whole trip taken entirely in the imagination: pre-flight jitters that lose to butterflies, a maximalist tourist itinerary of Shinkansen and 7-Eleven ramen, capybara onsen, a Miyazaki mountain hike, beer in the Sapporo snow. The chorus stacks gonna go to Japan until belief almost feels like booking. Then the brake: after the Japanese builds (日本に行きたい, I want to go to Japan), the last line lands, けど今できない, but I can't right now. The whole song is future tense, and that's the honest crack in it.

The gap closes on I, recorded partly on the Tōkaidō, San'yō, and Tōhoku Shinkansen. On Moonlight Beach Japan is still the horizon. Points forward to MÖB and the Möbius tag on the closer too.

Before the Shinkansen sessions: Luke's first night in Japan — landing on his birthday — ended in a four-hour Kanda jam (20+ songs with local musicians). Instagram TV archive.
