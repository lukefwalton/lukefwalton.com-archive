Content

Track two on The Other Side (2009, ~5:52). Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia cover of Donavon Frankenreiter’s “Move By Yourself”: title track from Move by Yourself (2006, Lost Highway).

High-school blues band lineup (parallel to Mannequin / pre–Luke Walton Band), not The Luke Walton Band USC cohort. Frankenreiter’s original is surf-adjacent funk-soul independence anthem (you've got to move by yourself); Walton’s EP slots it between original Preschool and two more covers.
