Content

Companion to AFTER-PARTY on the same chapter EP. Where the title track still had verses (capacity, shortcuts, bruises, the cigarette that wasn't), this one keeps only the pre-chorus rule and runs the chorus four times back to back.

Same hook, nothing good ever happens after 2 am, same answer, not ready yet, no new scenery. The night has outlasted the plot. That's the joke and the point: the party doesn't need another verse when the refusal is the whole song.
