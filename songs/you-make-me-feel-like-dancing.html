Cover of Leo Sayer’s 1976 hit “You Make Me Feel Like Dancing”: standalone single (February 7, 2025, Beformer) per Catalog. Not a KŌAN LP track; a post-MÖBIUS-cycle cover drop in the same lane as when it's over (Sugar Ray) and other distro singles.

Luke Francis Walton’s version keeps the dance-floor DNA of the original, falsetto joy, handclap energy, filtered through bedroom-pop / funk production. No original lyrics on cover pages.
