Content

Track ten on $WAMI$, Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2 continues the snack-era branding from My Scoobert Snack, dropped in the middle of the twenty-track collage between Spice Up the Bedroom With Spirit Cuffs and Adonde Eres. The $WAMI$ album notes call out Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2 alongside What a Velma, What a Night (Quickie) and Mother of Exile as proof the LP is remixing the debut world, not a clean second chapter.

Luke Francis Walton wrote, played, tracked, and mixed the free Bandcamp LP (May 9, 2018). Snack branding predates the post-pandemic Big Hug pivot, a recurring joke title before the character fully graduated.
