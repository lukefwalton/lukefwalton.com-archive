Content

Track four on Plague Beats, Vol. 2 (May 14, 2021), one minute, Luke solo. Among Us at peak meme (2020–2021), sus and emergency meetings and vent kills, turned into a full micro-song instead of a title reference.

It runs the whole crewmate arc in sixty seconds: lobby dread (and we just jettisoned a good man), the paranoia hook (you're looking kinda sus / I don't think I can trust / nobody but myself), and a near-escape to the cafeteria just as I die. Then the outro, I'm a ghost: dead, still on voice chat. It's the most plot on Vol. 2, the same character-monologue mode as Vol. 1's Birds With Arms, and pure pandemic indoor gaming beside the Masks and Monsters lockdown.
