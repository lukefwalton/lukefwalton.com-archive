Track fourteen on [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/), after [*What a Velma, What a Night*](/songs/what-a-velma-what-a-night/) and before *I Can Show You A Whole New World (on a Magic Carpet Ride)*. [**$WAMI$ album notes**](/albums/wami/) credit **Louis Cole** on the drum-and-bass moments here and on *Vegeta Is Bulma's Bad Boi*: one of the few outside players on an LP Luke otherwise tracked alone in a bathrobe.

“Connections” reads as a network pun; it sits in the late-album run of the collage without being a featured-vocal cut, more groove interlude with guest drums than hip-hopera.
