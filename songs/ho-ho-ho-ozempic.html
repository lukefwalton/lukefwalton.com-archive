Ho Ho Ho (Ozempic), standalone holiday meme single, separate from A Very Doobert Christmas, Vol. 1 (2023). Catalog lists it on an indiemono holiday compilation (2025), title pairs Santa’s ho ho ho with Ozempic / GLP-1 cultural moment as punchline.

Not part of the MÖBIUS LP cycle; sits with other one-off holiday drops (Merry Christmas from the Beach, Christmas covers) as Scoobert’s parallel Christmas lane.
