Content

Track fifteen on KŌAN, KŌAN C (June 30, 2022), after No Worries, Yes Worries. Luke solo; YouTube video.

Imposter syndrome dressed up as a pep talk. The self-help imperatives stack up (lighten up, go deal with it, laugh it up), and the title hook deflates all of them. Underneath is the exit fantasy: how freeing it would be to quit right here.

The anxiety gets specific in the recurring-dream verse: you graduate, get the job, and they find out you were short two credits and never earned the degree. The ambitions crash the same way, the dream undone by the machine it depends on (my laptop crashes and there's no autotune to save me).

Same KŌAN C identity thread as What Makes You You and 無門関: if you're faking it, who's the you underneath? The Bent Stick takes the other side, on standing behind what you can't prove.
