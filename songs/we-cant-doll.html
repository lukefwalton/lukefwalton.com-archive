Content

KOMAGOME is YUUKA and CHIHIRO (ex-つぼみ大革命), Rolling Stone Japan and ORICON frame WE CAN'T DOLL (August 27, 2025) as MANA and KANA's first produced artist after CHAI's breakup: publicly legible as their first post-CHAI production lane, immediately before the OKAME relaunch (September 2025). B-PASS: KOMAGOME asked MANA/KANA to help aim for a new KAWAII; the result is underground pop (punk, funk, pop fused).

Uta-Net lists 週刊ナイナイミュージック ending-theme placement, 作詞 MANA・KANA, and 作曲 Scoobert Doobert・MANA・KANA (composer index · UtaTen); Apple Music credits Scoobert Doobert as arranger and composer. Luke Francis Walton produced. Official MV · /music/#selected-videos. Follow-up: CHAHHAN (2026 · co-produced · mixed · performed). Walton mixed OKAME DEBUT in the same MANA/KANA arc, the living Japanese pop graph, not a dead CHAI footnote.
