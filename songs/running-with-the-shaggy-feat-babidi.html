Content

Track seventeen on $WAMI$, Running with the Shaggy with Babidi on vocals, after Mother of Exile and before the SmarterChild pair (Smarter Child (Slight Return), Rest in Peace Smarter Child). Adjacent to Shaggy Connections (Louis Cole drums, no Babidi feature).

Not a Dragon Ball $d album cut: it lives on the 2018 collage LP that predates the hip-hopera. Babidi’s featured run here sets up the fuller cartoon-rap arc on the 2020 record.
