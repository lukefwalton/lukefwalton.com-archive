Content

Domestic collab single: Scoobert Doobert with India Thieriot (LA singer-songwriter). Released October 11, 2024 on Beformer; Nagamag heard carefree, light-footed indie pop: room to relax away from city noise, soft distinctive vocals, love everywhere and the wish to actually live inside that feeling.

Sits between the I LP cycle and later 2025 singles, a standalone duet, not on I, but in the same u-shaped love-song neighborhood as time with u and that's how u know i love u. Luke Francis Walton on the Scoobert side (write, produce, mix).

India Thieriot is credited as co-vocalist on streaming metadata; no public press article found naming a split beyond the artist line. Video: YouTube.
