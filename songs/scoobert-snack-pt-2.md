Track ten on [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/), **Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2** continues the snack-era branding from [*My Scoobert Snack*](/songs/my-scoobert-snack/), dropped in the middle of the twenty-track collage between *Spice Up the Bedroom With Spirit Cuffs* and [*Adonde Eres*](/songs/adonde-eres-feat-babidi-gokudaxij/). The [$WAMI$ album notes](/albums/wami/) call out *Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2* alongside *What a Velma, What a Night (Quickie)* and *Mother of Exile* as proof the LP is remixing the debut world, not a clean second chapter.

Luke Francis Walton wrote, played, tracked, and mixed the free Bandcamp LP (**May 9, 2018**). Snack branding predates the post-pandemic [*Big Hug*](/albums/big-hug/) pivot, a recurring joke title before the character fully graduated.
