Content

FEiN single (2021), co-written by Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward. Mixed and mastered by Frank Rosato, same Woodcliff engineer chain as Behave, Bodies (Oct 2021) uses Cary Singer / Riley Knapp per Discogs. Not on Little Homes (2016); later-era FEiN, same duo.

Avoidance as a habit. The hook keeps asking does it work, or is it eating ya because the answer never arrives, and the oh my god refrains are social panic, speaking without confessing, keeping the wrong thing visible but unnamed.

The title verse reframes self-destruction as care: digging a grave but calling it shelter, with delicate working as both insult and diagnosis. The second pass finally admits the trade, the light is worth the bruises, a contradiction the song holds without resolving. Pairs with Bodies (2021, choking on too much) and Behave (2019, commanded loyalty).
