Track one on [*Plague Beats, Vol. 2*](/albums/plague-beats-vol-2/) (**May 14, 2021**), one minute, Luke solo; sequel to [*Plague Beats, Vol. 1*](/albums/plague-beats-vol-1/) (2020). The title is the entire concept: a loop that claims to improve each pass. One unit (*better every loop / b-better every loop / yeah*), sixteen times, the stutter doing breakbeat duty as loop restart.

The joke is that repetition is supposed to get better and never bothers to prove it. Vol. 2 continues with [*Instant Regret*](/songs/instant-regret/), [*Cringe*](/songs/cringe/), [*Looking Kinda Sus (Among Us)*](/songs/looking-kinda-sus-among-us/), and [*Shitty Robots*](/songs/shitty-robots/).
