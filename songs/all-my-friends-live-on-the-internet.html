Content

Track twenty on KŌAN, KŌAN C closer energy, also a standalone single with video.

The hook is a contradiction the song keeps on purpose: all my friends live on the internet, but there's nothing virtual about you. The relationships are real, the geography isn't. Would you be mad if I said I never really wanna meet ya in-person isn't antisocial; it's the fear that flesh would kill the vibe you already have. You're probably my best friend while my mom thinks I'm pathetic / I think she's getting old.

Then infrastructure as comedy: Wi-Fi down, DSP throttling, slap the router, neighbor's password is Secret123. Lucky me. A platonic love song for people who only exist as voice and memes and late-night co-op, and it treats that as worth celebrating. Pairs with 5G BABY (always-on pocket love) and 4:20 pm (shared lonely rock). KŌAN C cluster with Who Am I Really Fooling Anyway, 無門関, What Makes You You.
