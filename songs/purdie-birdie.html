Content

Standalone 2018 instrumental (~1:15, SoundExchange). Pre-$WAMI$ scatter; the title puns Bernard Purdie, whose drum loops $WAMI$ later deliberately messes with (album notes credit Nate Smith, Efa Etoroma Jr, and Purdie samples).

Same ~one-minute distro habit as Ogilvie Maurice Hedgehog and Donald Trumpet. Instrumental; no lyrics page.
