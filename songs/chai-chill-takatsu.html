Content

Chill Takatsu: CHAI × TAKATSU-KING (TAKATSU-KING is Shingo Murakami of SUPER EIGHT / Kanjani Eight). Luke Francis Walton produced.

Released for television only, not on CD or commercial streaming. Same production era as WHOLE, 夢のはなし (MY DREAM), and ラブじゃん（ That's Love）; Illustrate Magazine (2022) names the CHAI and Murakami thread alongside Who Am I Really Fooling Anyway.

CHAI and Murakami also share a separate official collab Walton did not produce: だれかおるやろ feat. CHAI — theme for Murakami Shingo no Dareka Oru Yaro! on Lemino (2023). TAKATSU-KING performs; MANA and KANA wrote; CHAI featured. Unreleased on standard albums. See /with/chai/#murakami.

No Scoobert lyrics. No public stream, catalog credit only. See With CHAI · Catalog
