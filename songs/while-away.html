Content

Collaborating artist with TOMMASO, the Chicago-based artist, producer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist Tommaso Veronesi (also Tommy Veronesi in Chicago teaching/music contexts; Music House Chicago lists him teaching guitar, bass, drums, piano, ukulele, and rock band). While Away was released May 6, 2022 as TOMMASO × Scoobert Doobert (Spotify; Apple Music lists the same 2022 alternative single). Marmoset also represents it as While Away feat. Scoobert Doobert by TOMMASO: useful licensing/catalog context beyond DSP metadata.

Release week: @tommaso_music posted a presave carousel (May 2, 2022 · Evanston) with full credits on slide 2 — Tommaso Veronesi produced and played most instruments; Scoobert Doobert (Luke Francis Walton) on lead/background vocals, bass, and mix; Sean Burke acoustic guitar; Andrew Krull pedal steel; Riley Knapp master. Photo @thenicklanglois; video @marleykinkead. @beformer confirmed Today's Indie Rock (May 7, 2022). See Catalog: official playlists.

Same Chicago orbit as later Chan Fuze singles Veronesi produced and Walton mixed (2023).
