**Garvie**: **October 6, 2023** on **BigBoss Records**. **Self-produced by Garvie**; **mixed by Luke Francis Walton (Scoobert Doobert).** [Melodic Magazine](https://www.melodicmag.com/news/new-music/garvie-so-high/) and [The Pentatonic](https://www.thepentatonic.co.uk/single-review-garvie-so-high/) name Scoobert’s mix as part of the track’s finish, including breaking Garvie’s self-imposed “no reverb” rule for a more expansive sound.

Late-2023 single before [*Boring*](/songs/boring/) (2024). No Scoobert lyrics. See [With Garvie](/with/garvie/) · [Catalog](/catalog/#production)
