Content

Track twelve on $WAMI$, Adonde Eres (Spanish: where are you) with Babidi and Gokudaxij on featured vocals, between Scoobert Snack, Pt. 2 and What a Velma, What a Night. Gokudaxij also appears on later Dragon Ball $d cuts (Prince of All Saiyans, Namek Has Dragon Balls, etc.); here the pair is still in collage mode on the free 2018 LP.

Luke Francis Walton produced the session; the Babidi / J MESA orbit threads through $WAMI$ into the 2020 hip-hopera. See With J MESA.
