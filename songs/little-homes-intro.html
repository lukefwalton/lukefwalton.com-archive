Track eight on Little Homes (May 31, 2016): instrumental interlude (0:44 on Discogs) with Justin Klunk on baritone saxophone. Sits between Creatures (track seven) and Twenty-Three (track nine), the LP's structural hinge before the second-side run.

Same Tiny Giant → LMU → Frank Rosato Woodcliff chain as the rest of the album. ISRC TCACO1633184 (SoundExchange). No lyrics on instrumental pages.
