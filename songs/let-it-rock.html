Track three on The Other Side (2009, ~3:32). Luke Walton feat. Blue Suburbia cover of Kevin Rudolf feat. Lil Wayne, “Let It Rock” (2008), electropop / pop-rock / rap-rock. Not Chuck Berry’s 1959 rock-and-roll standard of the same title. High-school blues band context: three of four EP cuts are covers; only Preschool is Walton original.
