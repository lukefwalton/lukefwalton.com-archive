Content

FEiN single (2017, ℗ Tiny Giant), co-written by Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward. Mixed and mastered by Frank Rosato. Post-Little Homes 2017 cluster with LOVED, It's Alright, P.A.R.T.S., and All Mine.

The hook is a paradox: I'm not afraid of you / I'm terrified of your friends. Not the person, the network. Fear is the only fluency anyone has left here (fear is all I understand, stacked to a mantra), and the stretched cle-ea-ea-ea never resolves into a word.

The second verse pulls the title into immigration myth: put my hand on the golden door / native looks of disgust, the Statue of Liberty tableau turned hostile, the body pulled taut to expel it. Same register as American Man and LOVED, except here fear itself is the American product being sold back to you. It pairs with later Liminal (fear is not enough) and Stupid Forever.
