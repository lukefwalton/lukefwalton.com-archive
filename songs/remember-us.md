**Embody feat. FEiN (3)**, a Walton/Woodward topline on [**Embody**](/with/embody/) (**Karolis Labanauskas**)'s single **Remember Us (Extended Mix)** (**June 16, 2016**). [Apple Music credits](https://music.apple.com/us/song/remember-us-feat-fein-extended-mix/1115303247): **L.F. Walton** composer, **Brandon Michael Woodward** songwriter/lyrics, **K. Labanauskas** composer. [Official video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SEBylzLx55M). **Remember Us [Re-Edit]** (**April 23, 2021**, The Bearded Man): [Apple Music credits](https://music.apple.com/us/song/remember-us-feat-fein-re-edit/1561218086) lists **Luke Francis Walton** and **Brandon Michael Woodward** as **vocals**, plus composition credits for Walton, Woodward, and Labanauskas; **K. Labanauskas** producer.

Released **sixteen days after** [*Little Homes*](/albums/little-homes-fein/) (May 31, 2016), same FEiN summer: concept LP at Tiny Giant, then a deep-house feature on Armada Deep. **Radio:** [Don Diablo — Hexagon Radio Episode 045](https://soundcloud.com/hexagon/don-diablo-hexagon-radio-episode-045) (**Dec 9, 2015** SoundCloud upload) opens with **Embody – Remember Us (feat. FEiN)** as **track 1** — episode description claims broadcast in **35+ countries**, plus **Evolution on iHeart Radio** (USA), **Fun Radio** (France), and **SLAM FM** (Netherlands). Pre-release radio support ahead of the **Jun 16, 2016** Armada Deep single. See [With Embody (Karolis Labanauskas)](/with/embody/).

What FEiN brought to the slot is the move the song turns on: the bridge image isn't a jump, it's the clarity *from* the ledge. *No one will remember us* is not a nihilist toast but the *memento mori* that buys the dance floor. The second verse refuses comfort (*no need for you to comfort me / at all*) and exits on *at all*, no chorus return. Topline as philosophy, not hook factory.
