Joshoo × Scoobert Doobert single (2023). Co-written by Josh Shpak (Joshoo) and Luke Francis Walton per Deezer writer metadata.

Shpak is the trumpeter and horn arranger behind Peter Gabriel’s i/o era: album credits on Road to Joy and Olive Tree, plus i/o The Tour (2023) on trumpet, French horn, EWI, keyboards, and vocals (Wikipedia, Pitchfork). Earlier: founding Ripe trumpet player (Berklee funk/alt-pop). Joshoo alt-pop production and sync work: Shoutout LA.
