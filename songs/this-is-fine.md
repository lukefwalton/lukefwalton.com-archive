Companion to [*LOL*](/songs/lol/) on the same chapter EP. Where the title track had verses (the party dodge, foot in mouth, *philosophical songs never have answers*), this one keeps only the coping phrase and runs it until the narrative disappears.

It's [KC Green's burning-room dog](https://www.npr.org/2023/01/16/1149232763/this-is-fine-meme-anniversary-gunshow-web-comic) as a pop song. The middle break even fractures the line, *everything is* / *fine* / *everything is*. Same denial as LOL, just louder, the way [*AFTER-AFTER-PARTY*](/songs/after-after-party/) followed [*AFTER-PARTY*](/songs/after-party/): sequel as repetition.
