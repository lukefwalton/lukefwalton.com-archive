Track two on [*Dragon Ball $d*](/albums/dragon-ball-d/), **feat. Babidi & Gokudaxij** (**Taylor James / J MESA** and **Eric Radloff**). Luke wrote, played, mixed, and mastered; they sing the feature. Same duo as *Adonde Eres* on [*$WAMI$*](/albums/wami/) and later *Quarantine and Chill* / *Happy Birthday* on [*Masks and Monsters*](/albums/masks-and-monsters/).

After the peacetime blunt smoke of [*In Peacetime Goku Smokes Blunts*](/songs/in-peacetime-goku-smokes-blunts/), [**Vegeta**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegeta) arrives and the plot starts. The hook is parade music with announcer ad-libs over the top, and the verse compresses his whole backstory into a few lines: dead king, Frieza's genocide, *Nappa, let's go kill 'em all*. Saiyan ideology as bumper sticker (*we don't feel no pain / just Saiyan*), then exit stage right. The discipline the LP promised after *$WAMI$*'s lore dump.

Still no Frieza, no Goku fight, just entrance energy. Next: [*Oozaru Transformation / Don't Kill Him, Krillin*](/songs/oozaru-transformation-dont-kill-him-krillin/).
