On KŌAN, Scoobert’s cover of Earth, Wind & Fire’s “September.” The album is a four-part alt-pop koan (Zen paradox, Japanese songwriting, spatial sound, post-pandemic happiness); covers like this and Wouldn't It Be Nice sit beside originals such as Think About It and All My Friends Live on the Internet without breaking the cycle’s mood.
