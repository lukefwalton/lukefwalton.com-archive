Rees Finley — I Don't Want To Walk Away (2017). Public SoundCloud credit: recorded by Brandon Woodward and Luke Walton at Tiny Giant Studios. Guitars are Jake Noveck; bass Nick Petrou; drums Brandon Woodward — Luke’s role here is recording, not performed guitar. No music video on record (SoundCloud only).

Same Tiny Giant session lane as Dear Abby (FEiN production + Luke guitars), Hate To See You (recording), and American Champagne.
