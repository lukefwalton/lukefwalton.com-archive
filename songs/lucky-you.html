Content

FEiN single (August 24, 2018), co-written by Luke Francis Walton and Brandon Michael Woodward. Mixed and mastered by Frank Rosato (same Woodcliff chain as Behave and Infant). Not on Little Homes (2016); 2018 single cluster after Friend (June 2018).

A deal you already asked for, with the need admitted upfront (didn't you beg for a minute of relief) and a grotesque price tag attached: so what if it came with a mother in a cage. Little Homes theatrical cruelty applied to intimacy, where acceptance means swallowing the whole package. The chorus is sarcasm as care, pity dressed up as a VIP pass: you get invited to the party / cause you're looking so sorry. The title repeats like a host congratulating a guest who had no choice.

The bridge turns medical. Immediate release carries the pharma double meaning, instant relief against a record-label release date, and the helper becomes the drain: bleed me, six times, mantra as extraction. Same FEiN register as Behave (scripted loyalty) and Infant (misread safety), but here the hook is mock gratitude. Pairs with Friend as the other 2018 Walton/Woodward single on this site.

I Heart Moosiq ran the premiere with direct Luke/Brandon quotes, the strongest indexed FEiN quasi-interview on the public web. See /publications/ for the full FEiN press vs Q&A map.
