Content

Track 3 on OKAME’s first album DEBUT (February 4, 2026). 主題歌 (theme song) for the short film 餃子店で朝食を (Gyoza-ten de Chōshoku o), produced by apparel brand FAT CLUB: Natalie notes it screened alongside the documentary OK A ME REVOLUTION THROUGH MUSIC at Euro Live, Shibuya (Feb 11, 2026).

Official MV: full-animation video directed by chao! (KANA’s husband, illustrator); retro-animated treatment per Skream! (Mar 2026), also on /music/#selected-videos.

The Apple Music credits pane lists Scoobert Doobert as Mixing Engineer.

No Scoobert lyrics. See DEBUT album notes · With OKAME · Catalog
