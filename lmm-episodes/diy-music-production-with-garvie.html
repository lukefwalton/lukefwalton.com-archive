hostNote
In this episode of the Love Music More podcast, we welcome **Garvie**, a remarkably talented artist who merges traditional songwriting with innovative DIY production techniques. Garvie opens up about his journey in the music industry, from navigating the challenges of self-producing tracks to experimenting with unique artistic identities through masked performances. The discussion provides insights into the often complex relationship between artists and their work, including how the pressures of perfection can inhibit creativity. Garvie's approach to songwriting and production reveals not only the importance of individual expression in music but also how connecting with fans authentically can elevate a musical project. If you're interested in music production, songwriting, or simply the creative process, this episode is filled with actionable insights and inspiring stories.
