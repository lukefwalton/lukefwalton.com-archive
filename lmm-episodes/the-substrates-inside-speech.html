hostNote
Where does speech end and song begin? This solo episode follows that line — through inflection, stress, and pitch that change meaning without changing words, the blurry border between talk and rap, and **Mononeon**'s groove-forward clips that treat conversation like a pocket. I pull on **Diana Deutsch**'s speech-to-song illusion, cross-cultural comedy timing, and what happens when AI voice and auto-tune reshape the musical vocabulary we inherit from everyday talk.
