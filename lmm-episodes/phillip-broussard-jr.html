hostNote
**Phillip Broussard Jr.** has engineered and produced for the Red Hot Chili Peppers, Adele, Eminem, Muse, Slipknot, and Rick Rubin sessions: and the through-line across all of it is live capture: setting up the band so they can actually communicate with each other, running the room so the performance has somewhere to go. We cover the full production chain: pre-session prep (the day before matters as much as the day of), how he decides on drum sounds with the mix already in mind, when tape adds something digital doesn't, phase correction approaches, and what capturing authentic performances actually requires technically. It's a session-room conversation from someone who has done this at the highest level.
