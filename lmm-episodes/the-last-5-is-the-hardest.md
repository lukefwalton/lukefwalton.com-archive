hostNote: |
  I realized that the fear means I'm close, it's a good thing, not a bad thing. That inversion is what changed how I finish things. The gremlins don't arrive randomly; they arrive at the five percent mark, which is also the only part of the process where quitting would actually cost you something.
  
  I'm finishing a record while recording this episode, which means the stakes are real and the metaphors aren't abstract. I put the music away, hiked up a mountain, listened to the whole thing, ran back down, and jumped back in. That's not a productivity hack, it's what reset the relationship with the work.
  
  The episode is honest about where I am in the climb. Not at the top, still going up. Understanding what the gremlins are and why they arrive exactly when they do makes them easier to move through rather than around.
