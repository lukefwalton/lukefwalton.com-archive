hostNote
In this episode of *Love Music More*, we sit down with **Jon Sandler** from Great Good Fine Ok, an artist whose journey through the ever-evolving music landscape offers invaluable insights for both aspiring and seasoned musicians. Having collaborated with legends like The Chainsmokers and toured with notable acts such as X Ambassadors and Tove Lo, Jon reflects on the realities of sustaining a music career while savoring the process. He shares personal anecdotes and the organic evolution of his music, emphasizing the significance of collaboration in navigating the industry. We peel back the layers of Jon's experience, discussing everything from the intricacies of songwriting sessions in Los Angeles to the emotional ups and downs of touring. This episode is a heartfelt reminder that the path of a musician is as much about the journey as it is about the destination, and Jon's passion for his craft shines through in every discussion.
