hostNote
In this episode of Love Music More, we welcome **JW Francis**, a dynamic indie artist who has captivated listeners with his unique sound and heartfelt lyrics. We delve into his journey as an independent musician navigating unconventional touring paths, the intricacies of modern-day music distribution, and the creativity behind his new single, "Orbit." JW shares insightful anecdotes about performing during the pandemic, the fascinating process of writing songs for Valentine's Day, and his philosophy on being an artist in today's landscape. His candidness about his experiences reveals not just the challenges artists face, but also the joys that come with building connections through music.
