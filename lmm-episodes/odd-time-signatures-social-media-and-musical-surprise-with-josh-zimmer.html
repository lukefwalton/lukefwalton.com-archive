hostNote
**Josh Zimmer** (Project Atlantic) blew up on Instagram and TikTok by doing something deceptively simple: pointing at **odd bars** hiding in plain sight, a sneaky **3/4 measure** in the *Dragon Tales* theme song, micro-surprises in radio edits, the kind of left turn that makes your ears perk up for half a second. We trace his arc from a 13-year-old **Pink Floyd / Wizard of Oz** sync-up summer, through bass-led voice-leading, to why he makes those videos: pop is often **simpler than it looks**, and the people behind radio hits are not amateurs. Then **Project Atlantic**, social media as discovery (not replacement) for an indie career, and why rhythmic surprise hits hardest in the songs you least expect.
