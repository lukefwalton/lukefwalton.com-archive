In this episode of Love Music More, we dive into the inspiring journey of **Serena Sun**, a Chinese-Canadian artist who has made an exciting leap from Vancouver to London. We explore the challenges and triumphs she faced during this big move and how it's reshaping her music style and artistic identity. Serena's story is not just about relocation; it's about taking bold steps in her career and embracing the unknown as she crafts her upcoming EP. As Serena shares her experiences, from navigating the music scenes to collaborating with fellow artists, you’ll feel the adrenaline rush of pursuing dreams and the magic that happens when you take risks in life and art.
